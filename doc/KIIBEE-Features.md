Creators & Educators

Businesses

Organizations

🚀 Main Features of Kiibee
1. Sell Digital Products Easily
Monetize your expertise by selling content like videos, e-books, audio files, and tutorials.

Supports: Streaming and direct downloads.

Access: Choose between paid or free access.

Automation: Built-in delivery system handles file access immediately after purchase.

2. Built-in Payment System
Complete financial integration without the need for external providers.

Accepted Methods: MobilePay, Credit Cards, and Dankort.

Includes: Transaction handling, automated receipts, and customer access management.

3. Create Your Own Content Website
Generate a dedicated website or channel for your content in just a few clicks.

Customization: Full control over branding (logos, identity) and layout.

Function: Acts as a professional mini-storefront or media hub.

4. Centralized Content Management
Gather all your assets in one place.

Integrations: Import from YouTube, Vimeo, Dropbox, etc.

Direct Uploads: Host your own files directly on the platform.

Organization: Group content into collections and categories.

5. Flexible Access Control
Free: Publicly viewable content.

Paid: Behind a paywall.

Protected: Password-protected content for specific users.

Lead Gen: Email-gated access to build your subscriber list.

6. Email Collection & Marketing
Require users to provide emails before accessing content.

Export contact lists for newsletters or external marketing campaigns.

7. Analytics & Sales Tracking
Monitor your performance with detailed reports on:

Sales revenue and view counts.

Customer data and behavior.

Filter by: Day, week, month, or year.

8. Multiple Content Formats
File Types: Video, PDF, ePub, and Audio (MP3).

Compatibility: Fully responsive across Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.

9. Online Courses & Programs
Build structured learning experiences.

Create modules, series, and learning programs.

Organize content into logical collections for students.

10. Live Streaming & Events
Sell access to live-streamed events and real-time online sessions.

11. Security & Hosting
Localization: Content is hosted on Danish servers.

Compliance: Fully GDPR compliant.

Reliability: Secure storage and encrypted delivery.

12. Subscription Plans & Scalability
Tiered pricing based on the number of files and transaction fees.

Enterprise: White-label solutions with custom setup and dedicated support.
